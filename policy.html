Our commitments to quality, health, safety, and environmental responsibility on every project.

Quality Policy

Engineering that holds up to scrutiny

AKAL Engineering Group Ltd. is committed to delivering engineering, compliance, and automation solutions that meet the quality expectations of regulated manufacturing clients.

We achieve this by:

  • Applying GMP-aligned engineering practices from the earliest stages of design
  • Assigning qualified personnel with relevant industry experience to every project
  • Maintaining rigorous documentation practices to support commissioning, qualification, and validation deliverables
  • Continuously reviewing our processes to identify opportunities for improvement
  • Ensuring every deliverable is reviewed before it reaches the client
HSE Policy

Safety on every site we touch

AKAL Engineering Group Ltd. is committed to protecting the health and safety of our employees, clients, and the communities where we work, and to minimizing our environmental impact.

We achieve this by:

  • Complying with applicable health, safety, and environmental regulations on every project site
  • Providing personnel with the training and equipment needed to work safely
  • Identifying and addressing hazards before they result in incidents
  • Encouraging open reporting of safety concerns without fear of reprimand
  • Reviewing our HSE performance regularly and acting on lessons learned

These policies are upheld by every member of the AKAL Engineering team, from project leadership through field personnel. Specific certifications and audit documentation are available on request.
