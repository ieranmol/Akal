About AKAL

We work as your team — not just for you.

A London, Ontario-based engineering partner built for the realities of regulated manufacturing.

Our Story

Formed around regulated manufacturing, not away from it.

AKAL Engineering was founded to close a specific gap: regulated manufacturers need engineering partners who understand both the compliance requirements and the operational pressure of running a production line. We built our practice around that intersection.

From our base in London, Ontario, we work with pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and food & beverage manufacturers across North America — supporting projects from early facility concepts through commissioned, validated, running equipment.
